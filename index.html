About The Guard

Guild History

The Theramore Guard is a World of Warcraft Classic guild formed on the Grobbulus (NA) server in 2019 with Greyshield as the original Guild Master. The vision for the guild was to be a place for polite and respectful players to level, roleplay, PvP and clear raid/PvE content together in fellowship. We began with a core team of 20 raiders and a sister guild, Stormpike Militia, with whom we raided two times a week: Onyxia one night and Molten Core the other. Together, by the blessing of Jaina, we killed Onyxia, Ragnaros (MC) and then Nefarian (BWL).

As we progressed into the end of the WoW Classic era, new leadership came in the form of a GM board consisting of Desan, Nisharu, Poderick, Hollie and Montqueesh. With this visionary co-leadership board, the Guard cleared through Ahn'Qiraj. Upon mutual dissolution of the co-GM hierarchy, Poderick assumed the role of guild master of the Guard with Montqueesh as his right hand night elf throughout the end of WoW Classic into The Burning Crusade.

In September of 2021, Montqueesh took the reins of the guild and ushered it into a new era of prosperity and accomplishment. With support of several key officers, the Guard continued to clear content throughout The Burning Crusade into the Wrath of the Lich King.

In August of 2022, Montqueesh appointed Boarrior to the well-deserved role of co-GM. To this day, the Theramore Guard continues to operate under this leadership structure with distinguished officers including Holidaze, Anethyst and Frawl.

Raiding Excellence

As we began our adventure into the temple of Ahn’Qiraj, we quickly realized that we should have reinforcements. We were blessed to have numerous prominent members of <Sanguine> join with the <Theramore Guard> in a joint effort to kill C’Thun and ultimately Kel’Thuzad in Naxxramas. By the grace of Jaina, we killed the Old God and on March 14th 2021 and then we killed Kel’Thuzad together after.

The Theramore Guard progressed into The Burning Crusade where we cleared Karazhan, Magtheridon’s Lair, Gruul’s Lair, Serpentshrine Cavern, Tempest Keep, Hyjal Summit, the Black Temple, and Zul’Aman. Although we put up a valiant effort, we did not clear Sunwell Plateau until early Wrath of the Lich King classic.

As shown in the memories below, the Theramore Guard continues to clear end-game bosses in the most recent WoW Classic content.

PvP Resilience

The Theramore Guard has always been a thorn in the Horde’s side. Whether it is dominating in PvP battlegrounds, raiding Orgrimmar with , defending the Alliance’s home cities, or claiming the Tol Barad territory, the Guard continues to be active in the war against the Horde.
